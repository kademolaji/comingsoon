About us

We are here to kick off
intelligent medical solutions

Plexilife was created to provide solutions in various areas of the healthcare system; Public health delivery, Public Health Surveillance, Contact tracing, Medical data capturing and management and home delivery of personal medical supplies and health test-kits. Plexilife is an intelligent medical software, an all in one Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Practice Management solution designed to simplify access to premium healthcare service for all. Plexilife will be deployed as a SaaS (Software as a Service) platform that aggregates medical/healthcare operators to offer services to patients while also computing instant reconciliation and timely settlement of revenue generated from transactions for all parties involved. The fundamental goals of all healthcare systems around the world is to improve clinical outcomes, expanding access and optimizing clinical costs and efficiency.

The web based Plexilife eHealth system is designed to integrate the following modular functions into a seamless experience for all users.

  • Appointment bookings
  • Ordering health lab tests
  • A secure telemedicine
  • Digitisation of paper based medical records
  • Disease tracking
  • Medical Billing
